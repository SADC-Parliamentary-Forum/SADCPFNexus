Resolution 45/2023

Adopted 2023 Economic

Resolution on Digital Economy Framework

Adopted at the 52nd Plenary Assembly Session

Lead Officer

Dr. Sarah Mbeki

Deadline

Dec 15, 2024

description Resolution Text

Article 1: Infrastructure Development Recognizing the critical importance of digital infrastructure as a driver for economic growth, member states commit to allocating at least 2% of GDP towards broadband expansion in rural areas by 2025.

Article 2: Data Sovereignty Member states agree to harmonize data protection laws to ensure cross-border data flows while protecting citizen privacy rights, adhering to the Malabo Convention guidelines.

Article 3: Digital Literacy To ensure equitable access, a regional fund will be established to support digital literacy programs in primary and secondary education across the SADC region.
