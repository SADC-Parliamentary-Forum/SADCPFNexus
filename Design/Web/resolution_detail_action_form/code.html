Resolution RES-2023-894: Education Infrastructure Grant

calendar_today Adopted: Nov 15, 2023
gavel Mover: Hon. Sarah J. Mbeki
verified

description Resolution Text

WHEREAS, the Northern District has experienced a 15% increase in secondary school enrollment over the past two academic years, resulting in severe overcrowding in existing facilities;

AND WHEREAS, the Ministry of Education has identified a surplus in the Capital Development Fund for the current fiscal year that can be reallocated to urgent infrastructure projects;

BE IT RESOLVED by this Assembly that the Ministry of Finance is hereby mandated to immediately allocate the supplementary budget of $45,000,000 for the construction of 50 new secondary school classrooms in the Northern District.

FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Public Works shall oversee the tendering process and ensure completion within 12 months of this resolution's adoption.
